Common Questions

Before you reach out.

Where can I drop off plastic?

We're currently building our first collection points in Bloomington, IN. Send us a message and we'll let you know the nearest location and hours as soon as they're live.

What types of plastic do you accept?

We accept most common plastic types including bottles, bags, containers, and packaging. Send us a message with what you have and we'll confirm it works for our processing pipeline.

How do companies partner with Elpis?

We offer corporate supply agreements for verified recycled plastic, ESG compliance documentation, and impact reporting subscriptions. Use the form to start the conversation.

Can I host a collection point?

Yes ‚Äî we're actively looking for community partners including businesses, schools, churches, and community centers to host bins. It's free to host and helps your community earn rewards.

How do I earn credits?

Every pound of plastic you bring to an Elpis collection point earns you Elpis credits. Credits can be redeemed for cash, discounts at partner businesses, and rewards. Sign up to track your impact.

Are you accepting investors?

We are in the early stages of building Elpis and are open to conversations with aligned investors. Reach out via the contact form and select "Investment opportunity."
