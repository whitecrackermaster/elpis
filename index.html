The Problem

The box is already open.

Billions of pounds of plastic are already out there. In our oceans. Our streets. Our soil. And it's not going anywhere on its own. We know you feel it — that overwhelming sense that the problem is just too big. That nothing you do alone will matter. We built Elpis for exactly that feeling.

How It Works

Simple. Intentional. Powerful.

01

Collect

Gather your plastic waste at home, work, or school. Every piece counts — nothing is too small.

02

Drop Off

Bring it to your nearest Elpis collection point. We make it easy and accessible for your community.

03

Earn

Receive Elpis credits for every pound you bring in. Redeem them for cash, discounts, and rewards.

04

Transform

Your plastic is sorted, verified, and sold to manufacturers who need recycled content to build better products.

  • Impact reporting for shareholders
  • Proof of circularity for sustainability goals
  • Subscription based accountability platform
