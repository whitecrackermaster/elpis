Our Mission

We are the hope that remained.

Elpis exists because the damage is real, the plastic is out there, and someone had to build the system to bring it back.

The Origin

Why we named ourselves after hope.

In Greek mythology, Pandora's Box contained all the evils of the world. When it was opened, hardship, suffering, and destruction escaped into humanity. But one thing remained inside after everything else fled ‚Äî Elpis. The spirit of hope.

We see plastic pollution the same way. The box has already been opened. Over 8 billion tons of plastic have been produced since the 1950s. Less than 9% has ever been recycled. It's in our oceans, our soil, our food. It's not going anywhere on its own.

We didn't build Elpis to pretend the box was never opened. We built it to be what remains ‚Äî the hope, the system, the solution that turns what was released into something the world can use again.

And just like in the myth, hope only works when people hold onto it together. That's why Elpis isn't just a business. It's a community. Every person who brings in a bag of plastic is part of something historic.
